Step 1: Complete Your Profile

The first step to a personalized journey is to tell us about you. This includes your existing skills, interests, and career goals. Your profile is the foundation for all future recommendations.

**Recommended Action:**

  • Fill out your profile completely.
  • List all your current skills, even those not directly related to your target career.

Step 2: Analyze Your Skill Gap

In this step, the platform uses your profile data to identify the skills you need to acquire. Our AI engine compares your existing skills to the industry standards for your target role.

**Recommended Action:**

  • View your personalized skills report.
  • Identify your biggest skill gaps.
  • Start focusing on the skills with the lowest scores.

Step 3: Learn Core Skills

This is the most critical phase where you will acquire the foundational knowledge required for your target role. Focus on hands-on projects and continuous learning.

  • **Courses:** "Introduction to APIs" (Coursera), "React Essentials" (Udemy)
  • **Articles:** "JavaScript ES6+ for Professionals" (MDN Web Docs)
  • **Projects:** Build a simple to-do list app with a REST API.

Step 4: Build a Portfolio

A strong portfolio is essential for showcasing your skills to employers. This phase focuses on applying what you've learned to real-world projects.

  • **Courses:** "Full-Stack Web Development" (Coursera)
  • **Projects:** Create a personal website, develop a clone of a popular app, or contribute to an open-source project.

Step 5: Practice for Interviews

This final step prepares you for the job market. Our AI coach will simulate realistic interviews and provide immediate feedback on your performance, helping you to build confidence.

**Recommended Action:**

  • Review common interview questions for your target role.
  • Use the AI Interview Coach to practice your answers.
  • Connect with a mentor for a live mock interview.
